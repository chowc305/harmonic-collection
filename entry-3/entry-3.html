I went to Xing Fu Tang with my friend, Audrey. We met on move in day freshman year and have been friends ever since. Even with busy schedules, she reached out to me and asked to hang out.
Overall, it was good catching up with Audrey after a while. Cherish your friendships and good boba. I first tried Xing Fu Tang 2019 in Macau. I remember being pleasantly surprised with the warmness and sweetness of the fresh made tapioca pearls. Brown sugar boba then became my go-to order for any boba place. Now I find it too sweet, but nostalgic. I do wish the tea had a stronger taste.

Was it worth the price?

Yes, good memories and good talking.
