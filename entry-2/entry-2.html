A Time of Togetherness

My friend Chelsea came back from Boston for the week, so my friends and I made time to catch up. We all met in high school, and the city was like our school yard.

After school, we use to go to the nearby bakery and get pastries and coffee, or get a cup of boba.

It was a sweet treat after a long day of school.

I really enjoyed the bounciness and chewiness of their golden tapioca

The lychee tasted nostalgic, and sweetened up the drink perfectly.

I would order this drink again.

4 out of 5

With my friends, we are able to share drinks and try different orders. I will order the peach green tea next time.
